# ChinaGateway Mobile App Installation

ChinaGateway is a Progressive Web App (PWA) that works like a native mobile app. Follow these steps to install it on your phone.

## iPhone (iOS 16.4+)

1. Open Safari and go to https://chinagateway.vercel.app
2. Tap the **Share** button (square with arrow up)
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **"Add"** in the top right corner
5. The app icon will appear on your home screen

## Android (Chrome)

### Method 1: Automatic Prompt
1. Open Chrome and go to https://chinagateway.vercel.app
2. Wait for the "Add to Home Screen" prompt to appear at the bottom
3. Tap **"Install"**

### Method 2: Manual Installation
1. Open Chrome and go to https://chinagateway.vercel.app
2. Tap the **Menu** button (three dots)
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"** or **"Install App"**
4. Tap **"Install"**

## Troubleshooting

### iPhone: Can't find "Add to Home Screen"
- Make sure you're using Safari (not Chrome or other browsers)
- Scroll down in the share sheet - the option might be at the bottom
- Update to iOS 16.4 or later

### Android: No install prompt
- Make sure you're using Chrome
- Try refreshing the page
- Check that "Install unknown apps" is enabled in settings

### App not working offline
- First, open the app while online to cache content
- Some features (like booking) require internet connection

## Updating the App

The app updates automatically when you:
- Open it with an internet connection
- The website has been updated

No need to reinstall from the App Store!

## Uninstalling

### iPhone
- Long press the app icon
- Tap "Remove App"
- Tap "Delete App"

### Android
- Long press the app icon
- Drag to "Uninstall" or tap "App info" then "Uninstall"
