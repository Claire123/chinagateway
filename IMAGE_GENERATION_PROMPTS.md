# Healthcare Institution Image Generation Prompts

## Photography Style Guidelines
- Style: Professional architectural/interior photography
- Lighting: Natural daylight, bright and clean
- Mood: Modern, professional, welcoming healthcare environment
- Resolution: 1200x800px or higher
- Aspect ratio: 3:2 or 16:9
